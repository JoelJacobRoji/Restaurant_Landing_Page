About Luna Rosa

Since 1985, Luna Rossa has been serving authentic Italian cuisine in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our family recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing you the true taste of Italy using only the finest imported ingredients and locally sourced produce.

Chef Marco Rossi, trained in the hills of Tuscany, leads our kitchen with passion and dedication to culinary excellence. Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.
